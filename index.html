Technology We leverage

Internet Of Things

We house the ability to transfer data over a network without requiring human-to-human interaction - get your groceries delivered to you without even having to speak to a store owner!

Fabrication

DASH is beautifully designed to integrate right into your house - no matter the aesthetic, no matter your preference. Its sleek, modern look provides you with the user experience you want.

Machine Learning

Using machine learning, we will analyze the data you unintentionally produce to give you information that you otherwise have to actively seek. Learn about your food consumption patterns, needs and wants.

Application development

Our application, which is coupled with DASH, gives you this information in an easy to use manner. Simply approve transactions, learn about your consumption and monitor magic happen!
